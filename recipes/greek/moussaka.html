Moussaka Recipe

A layered casserole with eggplant, ground meat, and bechamel sauce.

Ingredients

For the Meat Sauce:
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 pound (500g) ground lamb or beef
  • 1 can (14 ounces/400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
For the Vegetables:
  • 2 large eggplants, sliced into 1/4-inch rounds
  • 2 medium potatoes, peeled and sliced into 1/4-inch rounds
  • 2-3 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt
For the Béchamel Sauce:
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups (500ml) whole milk, warmed
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 large egg, beaten

Preparation Time

  • Prep time: 30 minutes
  • Cook time: 1 hour 20 minutes
  • Total time: ~2 hours

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce:
    • Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and sauté until softened.
    • Add the ground meat and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon.
    • Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, red wine (if using), cinnamon, oregano, allspice, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20-25 minutes, until the sauce thickens. Set aside.
  2. Cook the Vegetables:
    • Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
    • Lay the eggplant and potato slices on baking sheets. Brush both sides with olive oil and sprinkle with salt. Roast in the oven for 20 minutes, flipping halfway through, until golden. Reduce the oven temperature to 350°F (175°C).
  3. Make the Béchamel Sauce:
    • In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Stir in the flour and cook for 1-2 minutes to form a roux.
    • Gradually whisk in the warm milk until smooth and thickened.
    • Add nutmeg, salt, pepper, and Parmesan cheese. Remove from heat and let cool slightly. Whisk in the beaten egg.
  4. Bake:
    • Bake the moussaka at 350°F (175°C) for 45-50 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbling.
  5. Cool and Serve:
    • Allow the moussaka to cool for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving.

Enjoy this hearty and comforting Greek classic!
