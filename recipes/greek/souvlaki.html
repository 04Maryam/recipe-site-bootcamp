Souvlaki Recipe

Skewered and grilled meat, often served with pita and tzatziki.

Ingredients

For the Marinade:
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon paprika (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 pound (450g) boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces (or pork, beef, or lamb)
For Serving:
  • Pita bread
  • Tzatziki sauce (Greek yogurt, cucumber, garlic, lemon, and dill)
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)
  • Sliced tomatoes, red onion, and cucumber

Preparation Time

  • Prep time: 15 minutes (plus 1-2 hours for marinating)
  • Cook time: 10-15 minutes
  • Total time: 1.5-2 hours

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Marinade:
    • In a bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, red wine vinegar, garlic, oregano, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Marinate the Meat:
    • Place the chicken (or your choice of meat) into the bowl with the marinade and toss to coat.
    • Cover and refrigerate for 1-2 hours to allow the flavors to meld.
  3. Cook the Souvlaki:
    • Preheat your grill or grill pan over medium-high heat.
    • Thread the marinated meat onto skewers, leaving a little space between each piece.
    • Grill the souvlaki skewers for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the meat is cooked through and has nice grill marks.
  4. Serve:
    • Warm the pita bread slightly on the grill for a few seconds.
    • Serve the souvlaki with the pita, tzatziki sauce, fresh vegetables (tomatoes, onion, cucumber), and chopped parsley.

Enjoy your flavorful and juicy Greek souvlaki!
