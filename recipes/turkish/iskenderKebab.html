Iskender Kebab Recipe

Sliced lamb or beef served with pita bread, tomato sauce, and yogurt.

Ingredients

For the Kebab:
  • 1 lb (450g) lamb or beef, thinly sliced (you can also use chicken)
  • 1 onion, finely grated or pureed
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon (optional, for extra flavor)
For the Tomato Sauce:
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cups crushed tomatoes (or tomato puree)
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional for heat)
For Serving:
  • 2-3 pieces of Turkish pita or flatbread, cut into strips (or any flatbread of your choice)
  • 1 cup plain yogurt (Greek yogurt works best)
  • Melted butter (optional, for drizzling)
  • Fresh parsley or mint for garnish (optional)

Preparation Time

  • Prep time: 20 minutes
  • Cook time: 20-25 minutes
  • Total time: ~45 minutes

Instructions

  1. Marinate the Meat:
    • In a bowl, combine the thinly sliced lamb or beef with grated onion, olive oil, cumin, paprika, black pepper, salt, and cinnamon (if using).
    • Mix well to coat the meat in the marinade and let it sit for at least 30 minutes (or up to 2 hours) in the refrigerator to absorb the flavors.
  2. Prepare the Tomato Sauce:
    • Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat.
    • Add the crushed tomatoes and tomato paste, stirring well.
    • Add the sugar, salt, black pepper, and red pepper flakes (if using) to the sauce.
    • Simmer the sauce over low heat for 10-15 minutes until thickened and the flavors are well combined. Stir occasionally and adjust seasoning to taste.
  3. Cook the Meat:
    • Heat a large skillet or grill pan over medium-high heat and add the marinated meat.
    • Cook the meat for about 6-8 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the meat is browned and cooked through. You can also grill the meat on skewers if preferred for a more authentic flavor.
  4. Prepare the Bread:
    • While the meat is cooking, heat the pita or flatbread in the oven at 350°F (175°C) for about 5-7 minutes or until warm and slightly crispy.
    • Once warmed, cut the bread into strips or tear into pieces, and arrange them at the bottom of the serving dish.
  5. Assemble the Iskender Kebab:
    • Place the warm pita or flatbread pieces in the bottom of a serving dish.
    • Spoon some of the tomato sauce over the bread, allowing it to soak in.
    • Top the bread and sauce with the cooked meat.
  6. Serve:
    • Spoon a generous amount of plain yogurt on top or serve it on the side for dipping.
    • Drizzle melted butter (optional) over the kebab to add richness.
    • Garnish with fresh parsley or mint leaves for added freshness.

Iskender Kebab is a beloved Turkish dish featuring tender, spiced meat served with tomato sauce, yogurt, and crispy flatbread. The combination of the savory meat, tangy yogurt, and aromatic tomato sauce creates a rich, flavorful experience. Whether served as a hearty meal or a flavorful lunch, this dish is sure to please anyone who loves Turkish cuisine. Enjoy!
