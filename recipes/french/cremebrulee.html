Crème Brûlée Recipe

A custard dessert with a caramelized sugar crust.

Ingredients

For the Custard:
  • 2 cups (500ml) heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup (100g) granulated sugar
  • 1 vanilla bean (or 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract)
  • 5 large egg yolks
  • Pinch of salt
For the Topping:
  • 4-6 tablespoons granulated sugar (for caramelizing)

Preparation Time

  • Prep time: 20 minutes
  • Cook time: 30-35 minutes
  • Chilling time: 4 hours (or overnight)
  • Total time: ~5 hours

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven:
    • Preheat your oven to 325°F (160°C).
  2. Heat the Cream:
    • In a medium saucepan, combine the heavy cream, vanilla bean (split and scraped), and salt. Heat over medium heat until the cream is hot but not boiling. Remove from heat and let it steep for 10 minutes. If using vanilla extract, add it after heating.
  3. Prepare the Egg Mixture:
    • In a mixing bowl, whisk together the egg yolks and sugar until pale and creamy.
  4. Temper the Eggs:
    • Gradually pour the warm cream into the egg yolk mixture, whisking constantly to prevent the eggs from cooking. Strain the mixture through a fine mesh sieve into a clean bowl to remove any solids.
  5. Fill the Ramekins:
    • Divide the custard evenly among 4-6 ramekins. Place the ramekins in a baking dish.
  6. Bake in a Water Bath:
    • Pour hot water into the baking dish until it reaches halfway up the sides of the ramekins.
    • Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the custards are just set but still slightly jiggly in the center.
  7. Cool and Chill:
    • Remove the ramekins from the water bath and let them cool to room temperature. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  8. Caramelize the Sugar:
    • Sprinkle an even layer of sugar over the surface of each custard. Using a kitchen torch, caramelize the sugar until golden and bubbly. Allow the caramel to harden for 1-2 minutes.
  9. Serve:
    • Serve immediately for a delightful contrast between the crisp caramel topping and the creamy custard beneath.

Enjoy this elegant and timeless French dessert!
