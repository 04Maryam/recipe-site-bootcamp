Gazpacho Recipe

Cold tomato and vegetable soup, perfect for summer.

Ingredients

  • 6 ripe tomatoes, peeled and chopped
  • 1 cucumber, peeled and chopped
  • 1 bell pepper (red or green), chopped
  • 1/2 red onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin (optional)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 2 cups cold water (or vegetable broth for more flavor)
  • 1 slice of stale white bread (optional, for thickening)
  • Fresh basil or parsley (for garnish, optional)

Preparation Time

  • Prep time: 15 minutes
  • Chill time: 1 hour (optional, but recommended for best flavor)
  • Total time: ~15 minutes (plus chilling time)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Vegetables:
    • Start by peeling and chopping the tomatoes, cucumber, bell pepper, and red onion into rough chunks.
    • Mince the garlic.
  2. Blend the Ingredients:
    • Place the tomatoes, cucumber, bell pepper, red onion, and garlic in a blender or food processor.
    • Add the olive oil, red wine vinegar, and ground cumin (if using).
    • If you prefer a thicker texture, add the stale bread (soaked in a bit of water) to the blender. This step is optional, but it adds a nice creaminess.
    • Blend everything until smooth. If needed, add cold water or vegetable broth to adjust the consistency to your liking. For a thinner soup, add more liquid.
  3. Season the Gazpacho:
    • Taste the soup and season with salt and freshly ground black pepper as desired.
    • Blend again for a few seconds to combine.
  4. Chill:
    • Transfer the gazpacho to a bowl or container and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, though it can be made up to a day ahead for even better flavor. The cold temperature enhances the freshness of the soup.
  5. Serve:
    • Serve the gazpacho cold in bowls or glasses.
    • Garnish with fresh basil or parsley if desired. You can also serve it with a drizzle of olive oil on top for added richness.

Gazpacho is a naturally vegan, refreshing, and tangy soup made with fresh vegetables, perfect for a healthy summer meal or as a starter for a larger gathering.
