Tortilla Espa単ola Recipe

Spanish omelette made with potatoes and onions.

Ingredients

  • 6 large eggs
  • 4 medium potatoes (Yukon Gold or similar), peeled and thinly sliced
  • 1 large onion, finely sliced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil (for frying)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley (optional, for garnish)

Preparation Time

  • Prep time: 10 minutes
  • Cook time: 30-40 minutes
  • Total time: ~40-50 minutes

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Potatoes and Onion:
    • Peel and slice the potatoes into thin rounds (about 1/8 inch thick).
    • Slice the onion thinly as well.
    • In a large non-stick skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Cook the Potatoes and Onion:
    • Add the sliced potatoes and onions to the skillet, seasoning them with a pinch of salt.
    • Cook gently over medium-low heat, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking. The goal is to soften, not brown, the potatoes and onions. This will take about 20-25 minutes.
    • Once the potatoes are tender and cooked through, use a slotted spoon to remove them from the pan and drain excess oil on a paper towel-lined plate. Reserve a little oil in the pan for the next step.
  3. Beat the Eggs:
    • In a large mixing bowl, crack the eggs and beat them well with a pinch of salt and pepper.
    • Add the cooked potatoes and onions to the eggs and gently mix, ensuring the potatoes are well coated. Let this mixture sit for a few minutes.
  4. Cook the Tortilla:
    • Heat a smaller non-stick skillet (around 8-10 inches) over medium-low heat and add a bit of the reserved olive oil.
    • Pour the potato-egg mixture into the pan, spreading it out evenly.
    • Cook slowly on low heat for about 8-10 minutes, occasionally shaking the pan to prevent sticking. The edges will begin to set while the center remains slightly runny.
  5. Flip the Tortilla:
    • Once the edges are set and the bottom is golden brown, it's time to flip the tortilla.
    • Place a large plate over the skillet and carefully invert the pan to flip the tortilla. Then, slide the tortilla back into the pan to cook the other side.
    • Cook for another 4-5 minutes, or until the tortilla is fully set and golden brown on both sides.
  6. Serve:
    • Once done, remove the tortilla from the pan and let it rest for a few minutes.
    • Slice into wedges and serve warm or at room temperature. Garnish with chopped parsley if desired.
