Paella Recipe

Rice dish cooked with saffron, seafood, chicken, and vegetables.

Ingredients

For the Paella:
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 1/2 cups Arborio or Bomba rice (short-grain rice)
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon saffron threads (optional, but traditional)
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 4 cups chicken or seafood broth (hot)
  • 1/2 pound (225g) chicken thighs, boneless and skinless, cut into pieces
  • 1/2 pound (225g) shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/2 pound (225g) mussels, scrubbed and debearded
  • 1/2 pound (225g) clams, scrubbed
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
  • 1/2 cup roasted red peppers, sliced
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Lemon wedges (for serving)
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Preparation Time

  • Prep time: 15 minutes
  • Cook time: 30-40 minutes
  • Total time: ~50 minutes

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Ingredients:
    • Gather all the ingredients. Ensure the broth is hot and set aside.
    • If using saffron, steep the threads in a small amount of warm water for 5 minutes to release the flavor and color.
    • Season the chicken pieces with salt and pepper.
  2. Cook the Chicken and Aromatics:
    • In a large paella pan or wide, shallow skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
    • Add the chicken pieces and cook for 5-7 minutes, until browned on all sides. Remove the chicken and set aside.
    • In the same pan, add the chopped onion and bell pepper, cooking for 3-4 minutes until softened. Add the garlic and cook for another 1 minute, until fragrant.
  3. Cook the Rice:
    • Add the rice to the pan, stirring to coat it with the oil and vegetables. Let it cook for 2-3 minutes until the rice is slightly translucent.
    • Stir in the paprika and saffron (if using) for flavor.
    • Pour in the white wine and cook until most of the wine has evaporated, about 2 minutes.
  4. Add the Broth:
    • Pour the hot broth into the pan and stir to combine. Bring the mixture to a simmer.
    • Arrange the chicken, shrimp, mussels, clams, peas, and roasted red peppers on top of the rice, pressing the ingredients down slightly. Do not stir the rice once the broth is added.
    • Let the paella simmer over medium-low heat for 15-20 minutes, until the rice is tender and the seafood has cooked through. If the rice is not fully cooked and the liquid has evaporated, add a little more broth or water and continue to cook.
  5. Rest and Serve:
    • Once the rice is cooked, remove the pan from heat and cover it with a kitchen towel. Let it rest for 5-10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
    • Garnish with freshly chopped parsley and serve with lemon wedges on the side.

Paella is a delicious, festive dish that's perfect for any gathering. The combination of seafood, meat, and rice, all cooked in one pan, makes for a flavorful and satisfying meal!
