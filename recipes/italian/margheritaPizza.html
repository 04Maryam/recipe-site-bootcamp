Margherita Pizza Recipe

Classic pizza with tomato, mozzarella, fresh basil, and olive oil.

Ingredients

For the Pizza Dough:
  • 3 1/2 cups (440g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons instant yeast (1 packet)
  • 1 1/4 cups (300ml) warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
For the Toppings:
  • 1 cup (240ml) tomato sauce (preferably homemade or canned San Marzano tomatoes, blended)
  • 300g (10.5 oz) fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt to taste

Preparation Time

  • Prep time: 15 minutes (+ 1 hour for dough rising)
  • Cook time: 10-12 minutes
  • Total time: ~1 hour 30 minutes

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Dough:
    • In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, salt, and sugar.
    • Dissolve the yeast in warm water and let it sit for 5 minutes until frothy. Add olive oil to the yeast mixture.
    • Gradually pour the liquid into the dry ingredients and mix until a soft dough forms.
    • Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 8-10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
    • Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  2. Preheat the Oven:
    • Preheat your oven to 250°C (480°F) or the highest temperature possible. Place a pizza stone or baking tray in the oven to heat.
  3. Shape the Pizza Base:
    • Once the dough has risen, punch it down and divide it into two portions for two pizzas.
    • Roll out one portion on a floured surface into a 10-12 inch round. Transfer it to a parchment paper for easy handling.
  4. Assemble the Pizza:
    • Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
    • Arrange slices of mozzarella evenly on top.
  5. Bake the Pizza:
    • Slide the pizza onto the hot stone or baking tray using the parchment paper. Bake for 8-12 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling and slightly browned.
  6. Add the Basil and Serve:
    • Remove the pizza from the oven and immediately garnish with fresh basil leaves. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with a pinch of salt.

Enjoy the simplicity and vibrant flavors of Margherita Pizza, a timeless classic!
