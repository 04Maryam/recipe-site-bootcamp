Rissoto alla Milanese Recipe

Creamy rice dish flavored with saffron and Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups (400g) Arborio or Carnaroli rice
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 teaspoon saffron threads
  • 5 cups (1.2 liters) chicken or vegetable stock, kept warm
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup (50g) grated Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste

Preparation Time

  • Prep time: 10 minutes
  • Cook time: 25-30 minutes
  • Total time: ~40 minutes

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Stock with Saffron:
    • Warm the chicken or vegetable stock in a saucepan. Add the saffron threads to the stock and let it steep while you prepare the risotto.
  2. Saut√© the Onion:
    • In a large skillet or saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened and translucent, about 5 minutes.
  3. Toast the Rice:
    • Add the Arborio rice to the skillet and stir to coat the grains in the oil. Toast for 2-3 minutes until the edges of the grains are translucent.
  4. Cook the Risotto:
    • Begin adding the warm saffron-infused stock, one ladleful at a time. Stir frequently, allowing the liquid to be absorbed before adding the next ladle.
    • Continue this process for 18-20 minutes until the rice is creamy and cooked to al dente.
  5. Serve:
    • Spoon the Risotto alla Milanese onto plates or bowls. Serve immediately, garnished with additional Parmigiano-Reggiano if desired.

Enjoy this rich, golden, and creamy Italian classic, a perfect accompaniment to Osso Buco or enjoyed on its own!
