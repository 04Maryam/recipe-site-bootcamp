Ramen Recipe

Noodle soup with broth, eggs, and green onions.

Ingredients

For the Broth:
  • 6 cups (1.4 liters) chicken stock (homemade or store-bought)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon miso paste (optional, for richness)
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon chili oil (optional, for heat)
For the Noodles and Toppings:
  • 400g ramen noodles (fresh or dried)
  • 2 cooked chicken breasts, shredded or sliced
  • 2 soft-boiled eggs, halved
  • 1 cup corn kernels (cooked)
  • 1 cup baby spinach or bok choy
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced
  • Nori (seaweed sheets), for garnish
  • Sesame seeds, for garnish

Preparation Time

  • Prep time: 15 minutes
  • Cook time: 25 minutes
  • Total time: ~40 minutes

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Broth:
    • In a large pot, heat sesame oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sliced ginger, cooking for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
    • Add the chicken stock, soy sauce, miso paste (if using), and chili oil. Stir well to dissolve the miso.
    • Bring the broth to a gentle simmer and let it cook for 15-20 minutes to develop flavor.
  2. Cook the Noodles:
    • Cook the ramen noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. Prepare the Toppings:
    • Soft-boil the eggs: Place eggs in boiling water and cook for 6-7 minutes. Transfer to an ice bath, peel, and slice in half.
    • Prepare the vegetables: Cook corn kernels and blanch the spinach or bok choy briefly in boiling water.
  4. Assemble the Ramen:
    • Divide the cooked noodles into bowls.
    • Ladle the hot broth over the noodles.
    • Top with shredded chicken, halved eggs, corn, spinach or bok choy, and green onions.
  5. Garnish and Serve:
    • Add nori sheets and sprinkle with sesame seeds. Adjust seasoning with more soy sauce or chili oil if desired.

Enjoy a comforting bowl of homemade chicken ramen, perfect for a cozy meal!
