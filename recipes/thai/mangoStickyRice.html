Mongo Sticky Rice Recipe

Sweet sticky rice served with ripe mango and coconut milk.

Ingredients

For the Sticky Rice:
  • 1 cup glutinous rice (also called sticky rice or sweet rice)
  • 1 1/4 cups coconut milk (full-fat for creaminess)
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
For the Mango:
  • 2 ripe mangoes, peeled, pitted, and cut into thin slices
For Garnishing:
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds or mung beans (optional)
  • A few mint leaves (optional)

Preparation Time

  • Prep time: 10 minutes
  • Cook time: 20-25 minutes
  • Total time: ~30-35 minutes

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Sticky Rice:
    • Rinse the glutinous rice in cold water until the water runs clear (this helps remove excess starch).
    • Soak the rice in water for 4-6 hours (or overnight if possible) to ensure it cooks properly.
    • After soaking, drain the rice.
  2. Cook the Sticky Rice:
    • Using a bamboo steamer or a regular steamer, place the soaked rice in a cheesecloth or cheesecloth-lined colander. Steam over boiling water for 20-25 minutes, until the rice becomes tender and translucent.
    • If you don't have a steamer, you can use a rice cooker with a steaming tray or a pot fitted with a lid.
  3. Prepare the Coconut Sauce:
    • While the rice is steaming, combine the coconut milk, sugar, and salt in a saucepan over medium heat. Stir continuously until the sugar and salt dissolve completely. Do not bring it to a boil; just heat it until the mixture is smooth and well combined. Remove from heat once the sugar is dissolved.
  4. Mix the Rice with Coconut Sauce:
    • Once the sticky rice is done steaming, transfer it to a mixing bowl. Pour the coconut milk mixture over the rice and gently stir to combine. Let the rice absorb the coconut milk and become creamy. Set aside for a few minutes to cool slightly.
  5. Prepare the Mango:
    • Peel the mangoes and slice them into thin, even strips. Discard the pit.
  6. Assemble the Dish:
    • To serve, place a portion of the sticky rice onto a plate. Arrange the mango slices alongside the rice, or on top of it.
    • Optional: Garnish with sesame seeds or mung beans for a slight crunch, and a mint leaf for added freshness.

Mango Sticky Rice is a beloved Thai dessert that combines the creamy sweetness of coconut milk-infused sticky rice with the fresh, juicy flavor of ripe mangoes. It's the perfect balance of textures and tastes, and a refreshing treat after a spicy meal. Enjoy the harmony of flavors with every bite!
